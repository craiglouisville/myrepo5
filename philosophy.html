Philosophy

Dividend investing is considered a solid investing strategy. Dividend Stocks LLC goal is to grow a portfolio 8% per year, inclusive of growth and yield, by choosing stocks with this goal potential. Through solid investment decisions and a long-term approach, one can build a strong portfolio and live off dividends throughout retirement. Dividend Stocks LLC goal is to help individual investors review investments, based on six criteria, to reach retirement goals. Every great company has a bad day, and on those days, wise investors take note. There are thousands of stocks from which to choose – but far fewer “sleep at night” stocks that, over time, can help you achieve your ultimate goals.
