About

This app, Dividend Stocks LLC, is an approach to helping select long-term investment stocks. The dividend portfolio model can be an effective method to reaching your retirement goals. Diversification and thorough research about company fundamentals is critical to choosing stocks. There are numerous ways to invest for long term growth - Dividend Stocks LLC is just one method. Each week, Dividend Stocks LLC will post one dividend stock idea based on 6 criteria. Buying and selling stocks based on Dividend Stocks LLC posts is at your own risk. Once a stock is posted, it is recommended that you further research the company. The information in this app is free and Dividend Stocks LLC is not held responsible for losses on stocks.
